
  PROGRAM: xx5.f90

  xx5 performs the 3D strain analysis of an elastic-plastic von Mises solid. 
  This program is based on Chapter 6 in Smith I.M. and Griffiths D.V, 
  "Programming the Finite Element Method", 4th Edition, Wiley, 2004.

    Usage: xx5 <job_name>
